Overview

MD. MURSHADUL HOQUE received the B. Sc. degree (Honors) in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from the Chittagong University of Engineering and Technology (CUET), Bangladesh, in 2006, and the M. Sc. degree (by Research) in Electrical, Electronic and Systems Engineering from the National University of Malaysia (UKM), Malaysia, in 2016. Since 2020, he has been pursuing his Ph. D. degree in Electrical and Computer Systems Engineering at Monash University, Australia. In 2007, he joined as a Lecturer in the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Chittagong, Chittagong 4331, Bangladesh, and later on he was promoted to Assistant Professor in 2010. He is currently an Associate Professor with the same Department. His research interests include power electronics, power converters, energy systems, RE & DE integration, energy management, power system stability, machine drives and control, and smart intelligent systems. He has received a number of gold awards for his innovative research in ITEX, MTE and PERINTIS, excellent thesis award, best student paper award and many scholarships including Commonwealth Scholarship. Besides, He is continuously serving as a potential reviewer in many high ranked journals of IEEE, Elsevier, AIP, Wiley, Springer, MDPI publishers, and International Conferences.
